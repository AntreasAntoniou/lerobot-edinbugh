Gallery & Stories

Reliving the best moments from our hackathon weekend.

The Great Calibration Race

On Saturday morning, the energy was electric as teams unboxed their SO-101 kits. A friendly, unspoken race began: who could be the first to successfully calibrate both arms? Team 'Robo-Rangers' took an early lead, but a classic port mix-up on the leader arm cost them valuable minutes, allowing 'The Bit-Builders' to clinch the first fully teleoperated movement of the day!

The SmolVLA Prompting Breakthrough

After the prompt engineering workshop, there was a noticeable shift. Teams went from basic commands to crafting beautifully specific instructions. The "aha!" moment came when one team realized that telling the robot *what not to do* was as powerful as telling it what to do, using negative phrasing in their prompts to avoid common failure modes.
